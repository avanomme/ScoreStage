## Description

ScoreStage is a professional-grade digital sheet music reader designed for performing musicians, music teachers, and conductors. Import, organize, annotate, and perform from your entire music library on iPhone, iPad, and Mac.

**Your Music Library, Elevated**
Import PDFs from Files, iCloud Drive, or drag and drop. Organize scores with collections, tags, and smart search. Browse by composer, genre, difficulty, or instrumentation.

**Perform with Confidence**
Full-screen Performance Mode strips away distractions and prevents accidental touches. Turn pages with tap zones, swipe gestures, Bluetooth pedals, or hands-free head and eye tracking.

**Annotate Like a Pro**
Mark up scores with pen, pencil, highlighter, stamps, and text. Use multiple annotation layers for teacher notes, performer markings, and rehearsal reminders. Export annotated PDFs to share.

**Playback & Practice**
Import MusicXML scores for notation-aware playback with tempo control, looping, transposition, and a per-part mixer. Practice with MIDI keyboard input comparison and experimental score following.

**Sync Everywhere**
iCloud sync keeps your library, annotations, and setlists up to date across all your devices. Use Handoff to seamlessly continue on another device.

**Built for Musicians**
- Setlist management for gigs, services, and rehearsals
- Rehearsal mark navigation and jump links (D.C., D.S., Coda)
- Score families: link full score, parts, and alternate editions
- Device linking for two-page spread or conductor/performer mirroring
- Full VoiceOver and accessibility support
